Both balances are theorems. The four areas are the rank‑one array [a  c] ⊗ [b  d], so the cross‑products ab·cd and ad·cb are each abcd — equal for all P (the multiplicative, Turn‑side invariant; verified to floating‑point noise). The complements ad and cb are equal exactly when P lies on the diagonal, i.e. a : b = c : d — Euclid I.43, the additive (Reach‑side) special case.
